Profile

Gru, a super villain, seeks to steal the Moon to prove his dominance. He adopts three orphaned girls, forming an unexpected bond that challenges his villainous ways. In a climactic showdown with his rival, Vector, Gru risks it all to save the girls and reclaim the Moon.
